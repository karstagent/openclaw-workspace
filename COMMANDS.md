# COMMANDS.md - Quick Interface Commands

## Quick Status Checks

**What are you doing?**
- Just ask: "status" or "what are you working on?"
- I'll pull from STATUS.md and give you real-time update

**Show me costs**
- Ask: "costs" or "burn rate" or "budget"
- I'll show token usage, $ spent, budget remaining

**What did you do today/overnight?**
- Ask: "summary" or "what did you accomplish?"
- I'll generate briefing from activity logs

**Show me the work**
- Ask: "show me [project]" 
- I'll summarize progress on specific projects

## Quick Commands

### For checking on me:
- `status` - Current work status
- `costs` - Token/cost breakdown
- `summary` - Daily/overnight accomplishments
- `logs` - Recent activity log

### For directing me:
- `pause` - Stop current work, await instructions
- `continue` - Resume work where I left off
- `priority: [task]` - Shift focus immediately
- `background: [task]` - Spawn sub-agent for it

### For visibility:
- `explain [decision]` - Why I did something
- `show [file/project]` - Summarize specific work
- `list tasks` - Show current task queue

## Morning Briefing

Every morning when you wake up, ask: **"morning briefing"**

I'll give you:
1. What I accomplished overnight
2. Token/cost summary
3. Any blockers or decisions needed
4. Today's recommended priorities

## Cost Alerts

I'll automatically alert you if:
- Monthly spend exceeds 50% of budget ($100)
- Monthly spend exceeds 75% of budget ($150)
- Any single session burns >10k tokens unexpectedly

## How to Use This

You don't need to memorize commands. Just talk naturally:
- "What's the status?" → I check STATUS.md
- "How much have we spent?" → I check cost tracker
- "What did you build?" → I summarize recent work

These are just shortcuts for common questions.

---

**Philosophy:** You shouldn't have to dig through files. Ask me, I'll pull the data and summarize it clearly.
